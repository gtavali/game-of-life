Conway's Game Of Life

Developed by Gabor Tavali

Start the game to choose a pattern from the list or create your own by click on board. Anytime you can save your Generation with a name. 
To simulate the life of your generation click on 'Play' or 'Next Generation' to check it step-by-step. 
You can clear the board anytime and if you want, change the size of it. Some patterns require different board size.
